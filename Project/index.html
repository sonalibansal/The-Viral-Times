The Viral Times


A comparitive study of youth-based events

Problem Statement

Our project, The Viral Times, aims at finding the most viral page on a particular event on the basis of various parameters, namely, fan count, count of people talking about (a page attribute on Facebook ) and the count of check- ins. The popularity of top 5 pages on the chosen event has also been mapped yearly to display the annual popularity of the page.

Beneficiaries

General public: to know which page is still active  and is the most popular and should be followed to remain updated about the particular event chosen for analysis.

Marketing firms: to know which page, pertaining to the event chosen, has the maximum fan base, and hence, should be targeted to popularise it's brand. By this, the brand will be targeting a fine strata of audience which has the highest probability of having a bend for that brand. For example, Facebook users liking the auto expo page are likely to be fan of automobiles. 

Future Scope

The viral times is not only limited to events, it can also be extended to find the most viral page for a particular sitcom, any celebrity and many more. The scope of this project is immense.
